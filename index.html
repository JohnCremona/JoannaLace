Publications in print

The following have all appeared in Treasures of Malta, which is published three times a year by Fondazzjoni Patrimonju Malti. They are studies of late medieval works of art, predominantly in the museums and art galleries of Malta. By kind permission of the editors of Treasures of Malta, scanned copies of these articles are available here (except the first, which is an earlier shorter version of the first article listed above under 'Online Publications'): click on the title of each article to view these.

  • St. Paul's Shipwreck in Early Illuminated Manuscripts, Treasures of Malta, Vol. VI, No.3, Summer 2000, pp.5–10, 90.
  • The Dormition of the Virgin at the Cathedral Museum Mdina, Treasures of Malta, Vol. VII, No.3, Summer 2001, pp.13–17, 98.
  • The Archangel Michael, Treasures of Malta, Vol. VIII, No. 1, Christmas 2001, pp.45–50, 116.
  • The Holy Women at the Closed Door, Treasures of Malta, Vol. VIII, No. 3, Summer 2002, pp.67–71, 104.
  • A Madonna and Child with Saints in the Fine Arts Museum, Valletta, Treasures of Malta, Vol.IX, No. 1, Christmas 2002, pp.61–67, 104.
  • The Picture Book of St. Anthony: The Medieval Way to Tell a Story, Treasures of Malta, Vol. X, No. 2, Easter 2004, pp.51–56, 111.
  • The Presentation of the Virgin in the Temple: The Illustration of a Feast, Treasures of Malta, Vol. X, No. 3, Summer 2004, pp.37–41, 102.
  • Letter to the editor on The Langue of England on Vittoriosa, Treasures of Malta, Vol. X, No. 3, Summer 2004, p.84.
